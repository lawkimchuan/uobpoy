  • Bronze Award | US$1,000

Selection of winners

Judging

  • Entries will be judged by a panel of local and/or overseas artists/art connoisseurs/art critics/curators or other creative professionals appointed at the discretion of UOB. The decision of the judges is final. No correspondence will be entered into on this subject before, during, and/or after the Competition.
  • Artworks will be judged based on these criteria: message, creativity, composition and technique.
  • UOB reserves the right not to award any of the stipulated prizes should entries fail, in the judges’ opinion, to meet the required standards.
  • All award winners will be notified by e-mail and/or formal notification letter and/or phone by 11 November 2013.
  • Prizes will be presented to all winners at an award ceremony in Jakarta on 19 November 2013 at UOB Plaza.
  • The UOB Southeast Asian Painting of the Year award winner will be announced in an award ceremony in Singapore on 26 November 2013.

Exhibition of winning entries

  • All award-winning and shortlisted entries will be exhibited at the 3rd UOB Painting of the Year Indonesia Exhibition which will be held at UOB Plaza from 19 November to 19 December 2013.
  • The UOB Painting of the Year from Indonesia will be exhibited in Singapore from 26 November – 7 December 2013 at:

    72-13 Gallery
    72-13 Mohammad Sultan Road
    Singapore 239007

Return of entries

  • With the exception of the award-winning entries which will be exhibited, all other entries will be returned to the artist after the 3rd UOB Painting of the Year Indonesia Exhibition from 20-27 December 2013.
